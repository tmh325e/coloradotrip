Explore Estes Park & Rocky Mountain National Park
▼
9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
🏔️ Rocky Mountain National Park — Bear Lake / Sprague Lake Kid Friendly
Start with the Sprague Lake trail (easy, flat, under 1 mile) for stunning mountain reflections, then Bear Lake loop. Both are beautiful even in late March. Dress warm — trails may have snow/ice. → RMNP official site
Afternoon option
🦌 Yellow Wood Guiding — Wildlife / Photo Safari Book Required Kid Friendly
Naturalist-guided wildlife tour in RMNP or surrounding areas. Spot elk, bighorn sheep, moose. Great for Mabry! → Book a tour · Email to book
Afternoon
🚶 Downtown Estes Park Riverwalk & Shops Free Kid Friendly
One-mile paved path along the Big Thompson & Fall Rivers through downtown. Shops, galleries, Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory (caramel apples!), and souvenir browsing.
Alternative afternoon drive
🛣️ Peak to Peak Scenic Byway Free
Colorado's oldest scenic byway. Spectacular mountain views, aspen groves, historic mining towns. Route: Estes Park → Hwy 7 south → Hwy 72 → back via Hwy 36. ~2 hour loop. → Byway guide
